Nargothrond

Nargothrond (Sindarin IPA: [narˈɡoθrond]) was an underground Elven fortress and city that was delved into the banks of the river Narog in West Beleriand during the First Age. It was built and ruled by the Ñoldorin king Finrod Felagund, son of Finarfin.
