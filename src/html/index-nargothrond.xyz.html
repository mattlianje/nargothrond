FAQs

Logo?

The logo represents a stylized smith's hammer, of hybrid Dwarven-Elvish origin, possibly gifted to Finrod along with the epithet "Felagund" by his allies the Dwarves of the Blue Mountains. It also incorporates the three ill-fated silmarils wrought into Morgoth's crown of iron that indirectly led to Finrod's demise.
